Affordability

We know one of the biggest concerns of college students is paying for school. We see the many students who struggle with scholarships, paying out of state tuition, and purchasing textbooks year after year. That’s why we’re making affordability one of the pillars of our campaign. By providing resources to faculty to reduce the cost of their classes, students will be able to expect more, for less.

Accessibility

A significant number of student issues come from a lack of resources. However, this is not solely that we don’t have certain resources, but also that the resources we do have are not easily accessible to the students that need them. To help students, the second pillar of our campaign is putting the vast array of existing resources back into the hands of students. Greater access to resources can solve issues with retention and help students know what programs are offered on campus, programs put in place to address the needs of students, free of charge.

Accountability

The voice of students is an integral piece of what makes K-State what it is. We count on both ourselves and others to provide the best college experience possible through academics and campus life. Accountability is vital as we maintain the priorities made by student administrations in past years and cater to the changing needs of students today.
